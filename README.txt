Sell event tickets online directly through your WordPress site with TicketSource. An easy to use, self service box office system.

== Description ==

TicketSource enables you to sell tickets and turn your WordPress site in to an online event ticketing sales tool.

The TicketSource Ticket Shop plugin enables you to embed your TicketSource ticket booking pages directly in to your WordPress website, so that customers can buy tickets directly from you.

Whether you sell 10 or 10,000 tickets, revolutionise the way you manage and sell tickets for your events with our easy to use, self service box office system.

Sign up for an account with us and we'll provide you with immediate access to your personalised dashboard where you can manage every aspect of your event online 24/7.

Using our interactive event designer, you could have your event available to sell in minutes.

Our flexible service enables you to sell general admission tickets from a quota or individually numbered seats from an onscreen seating plan.

[Sign up for a TicketSource account to sell tickets online](https://www.ticketsource.co.uk/)

== Installation ==

1. Install the TicketSource Ticket Shop plugin via the WordPress admin dashboard

2. Sign up for an account with [TicketSource](https://www.ticketsource.co.uk)

3. Add your event to the TicketSource dashboard via the "Events | New Event" menu option

4. Locate your unique TicketSource Ticket Shop plugin code in the TicketSource dashboard via the "Events | Publicise Events" menu option

5. Add your unique TicketSource Ticket Shop plugin code to your WordPress site

6. Your customers can now book tickets for your event from your WordPress website.
